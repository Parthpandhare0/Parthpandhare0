# about_me

A bit about how I got here

I'm a CSE undergraduate at IIT Kanpur, currently in my second year. My interest in AI/ML started with a curiosity about how a few lines of math could recognise handwriting — and turned into late nights training models, reading papers, and entering every Kaggle competition I could find time for.

When I'm not training models, I'm probably solving a competitive programming problem, contributing to an open-source ML library, or arguing about whether a result is statistically significant. I care about building things that are not just accurate, but interpretable and useful.
